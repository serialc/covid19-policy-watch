COVID-19 EU PolicyWatch visualization

EU country COVID-19 policy responses

We are by now familiar with the daily graphs of COVID-19 country-by-country confirmed cases and deaths.

What's less well known is what policies are being implemented and their relationship to the number of COVID-19 cases.

These interactive infographics provide a temporal comparison of COVID-19 cases and policies for each country in the EU.

Policy data is from COVID-19 EU PolicyWatch database, and number of cases from CSSE, Johns Hopkins University
